### Improvements

[**Enterprise Tenant**] A cookie banner has been added to the Cumulocity IoT platform. The login page will now contain information about cookies and their purpose.

The cookie banner can be configured via the application options or via UI from the **Branding** page in the Administration application. For details refer to [Enterprise Tenant > Customization](https://cumulocity.com/guides/10.5.7//users-guide/enterprise-edition/#customization) in the *User guide*. [MTM-29923]
